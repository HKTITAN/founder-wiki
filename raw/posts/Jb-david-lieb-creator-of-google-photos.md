In this episode of The Social Radars, Jessica Livingston and Carolynn Levy talk to David Lieb, YC Visiting Partner and
founder of Bump, the app that evolved into Google Photos. Listen in as he talks about getting acquired by Google and
ultimately transforming his app into one of the biggest photo services in the world.
