The Social Radars (Jessica Livingston and Carolynn Levy) talk with Trevor Blackwell, one of Jessica’s fellow co-founders
at Y Combinator. He was also the founder of Anybots, where he created the first dynamically balancing biped robot, and
also worked on Viaweb with Paul Graham and Robert Morris.
